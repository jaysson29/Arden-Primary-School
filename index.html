Welcome To Arden Primary School

At Arden We Belive In The Value Of Every Child And Adult Who Enter Our Doors

On behalf of the pupils, staff and governors of Arden Primary School I would like to extend a warm welcome to you and thank you for taking the time to find out more about our school.

I hope you will find out through this website that Arden Primary School is a vibrant and exciting place of learning, as it has been in times past. We are building on the tradition of producing critical and influential thinkers who have shaped our world.

We deliver an exciting creative curriculum with a focus on achievement, while inspiring a love of learning and nurturing the whole child. Please do come and visit us - I will be delighted to meet you and proud to give you a taste of our wonderful school.
